Our Story

At LoveDove Weddings, we believe that love is a beautiful journey worth capturing. Our founder discovered a passion for photography while exploring life's diverse dimensions. Through the lens, we found a way to preserve the fleeting yet profound moments of love.

Our Mission

Inspired by the beauty of love, we created LoveDove Weddings to cherish and capture the timeless memories of couples on their special day. With expertise in photography and drone piloting, we lead the way in crafting stunning visual stories that celebrate the essence of love.

Our Vision

We're dedicated to delivering exceptional wedding photography that tells a unique story of love, laughter, and adventure. Our goal is to provide cherished memories that you'll treasure for a lifetime

Timeless Frames, Timeless Stories

Each frame we capture speaks volumes, telling a story that goes beyond time and generations. While others follow trends, we focus on preserving the authenticity of your special day. Our photographs are more than just images – they're memories that we create for a lifetime.

The Heart of Our Photography

There's no magic behind our stories – just passion! Photography is our driving force, and we believe that every wedding is a reflection of the love, hard work, and dedication of the souls involved. We strive to convey these emotions in their natural beauty, capturing the essence of each moment without losing the happiness and intensity behind them.

Meet Our Team

Led by our founder, Anandajith Baburaj, a licensed Drone Pilot and Chief Photographer, our team of experts is passionate about capturing the beauty of love. Anandajith is one of the select few in India to hold a drone pilot license, making him a true pioneer in aerial wedding photography. With a keen eye for detail and a love for storytelling, we're here to make your wedding memories unforgettable.
